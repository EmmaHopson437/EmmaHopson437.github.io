What Is TSA?

Technology Student Association
TSA is a student association that focusses on teaching students leadership through over 50 competetive events. These events range from fashion design, to coding and animatronics. There are several ways to be a part of TSA.

How To Join

At your school there is a TSA chapter. You can come talk to the advisor of your school's chapter to get more information about joining. If you do not have a chapter at your school you can either create a chapter or find a school near you.

Benefits of Joining

TSA teaches students real world skills to help them with professional development. They will learn to use devices such as their Apple Computers. They will also learn leadership and public speaking. All of which will help them in the real world.
