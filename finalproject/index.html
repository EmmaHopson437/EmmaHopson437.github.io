Fun Facts

  1. NASA stands for National Aeronautics and Space Administration
  2. It was first created in July 29, 1958
  3. It is an "independent agency of the executive branch of the United States federal government"
  4. NASA is a civilian space program and they are responsible for doing aerospace and aeronautic research
